Chan doan: KHONG CO DAU HIEU OVERFITTING/UNDERFITTING DANG KE

- RF v4 giam nhe 0.27% so v3 (91.54% -> 91.27%) do class_weight='balanced'

Nhan xet: Model hoat dong tot o muc ceiling cua TF-IDF. De cai thien them
can phuong phap bieu dien ngu nghia tot hon (pretrained word embeddings).

4.4 LSTM - 90.54%
------------------------------------------------------------

Kien truc:
  Embedding(8262, 64, trainable=True)
  -> Dropout(0.3)
  -> Conv1D(64, kernel=5, relu)
  -> MaxPooling1D(pool=4)
  -> LSTM(64)
  -> Dense(4, softmax)

Training curve (46 epochs, EarlyStopping patience=10):

  Epoch  | Train Acc | Val Acc  | Ghi chu
  -------+-----------+----------+---------------------------
  1-12   | 45-58%    | 44-58%   | STUCK - khoi dong cham
  13     | 67.9%     | 72.8%    | But toc dot ngot
  14     | 83.2%     | 84.0%    | Nhay vot lan 2
  20     | 95.8%     | 89.3%    | Diverge bat dau
  28     | 97.5%     | 90.1%    | LR: 1e-3 -> 5e-4
  35     | 98.7%     | 90.5%    | Val peak
  36 (*) | 98.6%     | 90.5%    | BEST EPOCH (restored)
  43-46  | 99.0%     | 90.3%    | LR da giam 4 lan, val plateau
  46     |    -      |    -     | EarlyStopping -> restore E36

Chan doan: OVERFITTING RO RANG TU E20 TRO DI

- Gap cuoi: Train 98.6% vs Val 90.5% = 8.1% -> overfitting.
- Loss ratio: Val loss (0.453) / Train loss (0.049) = 9.2x
  -> model memorize training data.
- 12 epoch stuck (E1-E12): Dac trung cua Conv1D + MaxPool truoc LSTM.
  Lop Conv can nhieu thoi gian hoc filter phu hop tren embedding 64 chieu.
  Sau khi Conv hoi tu (E13), LSTM hoc rat nhanh (E13->E14: +15%).
- ReduceLROnPlateau giam LR 4 lan nhung val accuracy khong tang them
  -> da cham tran.
- EarlyStopping hoat dong dung, lay model tot nhat o E36.

Nguyen nhan overfitting: Embedding 8262 x 64 = 528,768 tham so rieng cho
embedding layer, qua lon so voi 20,888 mau train. Model du capacity de ghi
nho toan bo training set.

5. CHAN DOAN TONG THE
============================================================

5.1 Tinh trang Overfit/Underfit

  Model | Tinh trang    | Bang chung
  ------+---------------+----------------------------------------
  RF    | Can bang      | Test metrics on dinh
  SVM   | Can bang      | Linear kernel + regularization ngam dinh
  LR    | Can bang      | L2 regularization tich hop
  LSTM  | OVERFIT       | Gap 8.1%, val_loss/train_loss = 9.2x
  GRU   | OVERFIT       | Gap 7.2%, val_loss/train_loss = 7.8x
  CNN   | OVERFIT       | Gap 8.1%, val_loss/train_loss = 8.4x

5.2 Phan tich Val Loss - Dau hieu Overfitting DL

  Tat ca 3 DL models co cung pattern:

    train_loss -> 0.04-0.06   (gan 0)
    val_loss   -> 0.44-0.45   (cao va khong giam)

  Val loss khong giam du LR giam 4 lan -> model da SATURATE, khong con
  hoc duoc thong tin moi tu validation set. Day la overfitting kinh dien.

5.3 Nguyen Nhan Goc Re cua Overfitting DL

  1. Embedding qua lon:
     8,262 x 64 = 528,768 params trong embedding layer duy nhat,
     chiem >80% tong params.

  2. Dataset nho:
     20,888 mau train cho model ~600k+ params
     -> ti le params/sample cao -> de memorize.

  3. Augmented data:
     Synonym replacement tao ra cac mau rat giong nhau
     -> model "nhin thay" bien the cua cung 1 cau, de memorize hon.

  4. Khong du regularization:
     Chi co Dropout(0.3) mot cho, khong co L2, BatchNorm.
